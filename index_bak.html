CosmoSteel is a leading supplier and distributor of piping system components to the Energy and Marine industries in Southeast Asia,

We offer an extensive inventory of approximately 10,000 line items across our three main product categories of pipes, fittings and flanges, comprising mainly of carbon steel or stainless steel products.

As part of our value-added services, we also provide machining services to customise our products according to customers' specific requirements.

Our products are sourced from a wide network of manufacturers and suppliers from Europe, US and Asia including Japan and PRC, who have a proven track record in product quality and delivery capabilities.

Product quality, timeliness and reliable customer deliveries are the foundation to the success of our business. As an endorsement of our quality assurance system, we were awarded ISO 9002:1994 certification for sales, supply and machining of flanges, steel fittings, tubings and pipes by Bureau Veritas Quality International in 2000, which was replaced with ISO 9001:2000 certification in 2003.
