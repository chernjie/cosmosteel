ISO 14001:2004 & OHSAS 18001:2007

Our Group has implemented an effective Environmental, Occupational Health & Safety Management System based on the processes below. The system is established towards securing environmental preservation, protection and prevention of pollution as well as instillation of safe work procedures and communication of our practices to all our employees and other concerning parties to prevent injury and ill health. It also provides the framework and mechanism to maintain and continually improve the Environmental, Occupational Health & Safety Management System in accordance with the requirements of ISO 14001:2004 and OHSAS 18001:2007 standards.
