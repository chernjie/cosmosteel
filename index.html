CosmoSteel is a leading supplier and distributor of piping system components to the Energy, Marine, Water infrastructure and other industries in Southeast Asia.

We offer an extensive inventory of more than 20,000 line items across our three main product categories of pipes, fittings and flanges, comprising mainly of carbon steel or stainless steel products as well as a range of complementary and value-added services to serve our customers better.

Our products are sourced from a wide network of manufacturers and suppliers from Europe, US and Asia, amongst others, who have a proven track record in product quality and delivery capabilities.

Product quality, timeliness and reliable customer deliveries are the foundation of our business. As an endorsement of our quality and reliability, we have received world-class certifications over the years including ISO 9002:1994 in 2000; ISO 9001:2000 in 2003; and in 2009, ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001:2004 and OHSAS 18001:2007. In 2011, we were awarded the Fastest Growing Internationalising Award and in 2012, we achieved the bizSAFE STAR status.
