Buttwelding Fittings

We supply a wide range of buttwelding fittings with sizes from 1/2” to 60”, where ½” to 24” are seamless and above 24” are welded construct, which includes the following:

  • 90˚ Elbow (LR, SR, 3D or 5D)
  • 45˚ Elbow (LR, SR, 3D or 5D)
  • 180˚ Elbow (LR, SR)
  • Tees (Straight or Reducing)
  • Reducers (Concentric or Eccentric)
  • Caps
  • Cross Tees
  • Laterals (30˚, 45˚)
  • Split Tees (Straight or Reducing)
  • Barred Tees
  • Studends
  • Customised Bends
  • Long Sweep Elbow

Our material range includes carbon steel, low temp carbon steel, stainless steel, duplex, super duplex steel, high yield, low alloys, copper-nickel, nickel alloys and titanium.
