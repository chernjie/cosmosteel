Forged Fittings

We supply a wide range of forged fittings with sizes from 1/8” to 6” which includes the following:

  • Elbow (90˚, 45˚)
  • ST. Elbow
  • Tee (Equal, Reducing, Cross)
  • Union (MF or FF)
  • Coupling (Full, Half, Reducing, 3” or 4” Length)
  • Boss (Equal or 45cut)
  • Cap
  • Plug (Hexagonal, Square & Round)
  • Hex Nipple (Equal or Reducing)
  • Line Pipe Coupling
  • Bull Plug
  • Reducing Bushing
  • Reducing Insert

Our material range includes carbon steel, low temp carbon steel, stainless steel, duplex, super duplex steel, high yield, low alloys, copper-nickel, nickel alloys and titanium.
